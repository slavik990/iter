NANOSHAPE

NANOSHAPE is a new division of AMOS that specializes in extreme precision machining of complex parts in various materials

NANOSHAPE therefore invested in most advanced manufacturing technologies. Our "state of the art" machines operate in a perfectly stable temperature and vibration free environment.

NANOSHAPE's mission is to allow our customers to design new shapes and consider the machining of extremely hard materials.

NANOSHAPE is working in close collaboration with the other divisions of AMOS to provide to our customers all the required expertises to realise the most challenging projects (mechanical design, optical design, metrology and optical testing, polishing, project management,...).
