TECHNEM Group

TECHNEM is a leading and innovative international group specialized in fluid technology solution through 4 subsidiaries. Operating worldwide, the Group has nearly 120 employees and generated sales of 12 Million euros in 2012.

JASPAR - http://www.jaspar.be/
Design and manufacturing of specific customized industrial valve(s) (products) for the nuclear and (petro)chemical industries. Design studies for modification of valves, assistance with engineering, on-site assistance, trading in valves products.

RISTERM - http://www.risterm.be/
Workshop and on-site maintenance of industrial valves, production, welding and machining, general mechanical engineering. :

POMMEE - http://www.pommee.be/
Specialist in human-scale pressure vessels and equipment, customized for pressurized fluid progressing or handling, filters and special equipment, mechanically welded assemblies, complete skid-mounted units.

TUBE INDUSTRIE - http://www.tubeindustrie.be/
Design and manufacture of (e.g. natural) gas pressure-reducing and metering stations, maintenance of gas pressure reducing stations. Industrial piping in a petrochemical environment and for carriage of fluids and gas.
