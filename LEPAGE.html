LEPAGE

Lepage designs, manufactures, installs and maintains equipment of industrial production enterprises especially in the Benelux and France

Lepage deals with

  • Erection engineering and feasibility studies
  • Study, execution and erection of steel structures and all kinds of steel installations
  • Industrial maintenance in mechanics, boiler construction, welding, piping,...
  • Expertise, transport and repair of machine parts
  • Production line transfer and equipment transfer, including electricity, pipework and civil engineering
  • Study, achievement and assembly of boiler parts and piping elements

Present since many years in the nuclear industry, we ensure, in collaboration with our customers, the implementation and maintenance of their facilities in compliance with all technical and quality procedures.

Examples :

  • Maintenance in mechanical services, static and rotating machinery, plumbing, welding, diesel mechanics
  • Active participation in projects "Belgian stress tests" (BEST) and "Long Term Operation" (LTO)

Our policy is quality, safety and well-being at work, and is reflected in the ISO 9001:2008 and VCA certifications**.
