ATLAS COPCO BELGIUM

Atlas Copco is a world leading provider of industrial productivity solutions. Our products and services range from air compressors, compressed air and gas equipment, generators, construction and mining equipment, industrial tools and assembly systems to related aftermarket and rental.

The compressor and generator range includes oil-free and lubricated stationary air compressors, portable air compressors, gas and process compressors, electric power generators, compressed air treatment equipment and management systems.

The construction equipment range includes hand-held pneumatic, hydraulic and petrol driven breakers, chipping hammers and rock drills, small to heavy rig-mounted hydraulic breakers and silent demolition tools and accessories.

As the innovators in all of our business areas, we are constantly driven to provide high quality, state-of-the-art products, which improve our customers’ efficiency, cut their production costs, and, at the same time, respect the environment.

Atlas Copco has been delivering in the past decades various equipments to the electric power generationsector and to the nuclear sector in particular in a wide range from stationary and portable compressors, pneumatic tools, and standard pneumatic and hydraulic demolition equipments to BROKK demolition robots with remote control, which are operating even in the radiation area.

Atlas Copco offers full service support for all of these equipments with the option of service agreements with full omnium coverage for total peace of mind of the customers.

“Atlas Copco is committed to your superior productivity through interaction and innovation”.
