KARL HUGO, AG

For more than 40 years, KARL HUGO has been amongst the leaders in the field of Mechanical engineering and machinery, including customized, special equipment or complete production lines.


We supply versatile equipment for a very wide range of productions sectors, from Energy and Mining, via Steel to Biopharma , Medical (IBA), Nuclear and many others.

Typical examples include test tables for laboratories, packaging robots, film cutting machines, grippers and manutention equipment, tooling stations and punching machines, special tanks and reservoirs, as well as a variety of accessories or modifications of existing processing or production installations.

We can also produce your machine according to your plans.

We carry out feasibility studies, design, manufacturing, assembling, on-site installation, commissioning and maintenance training.

KARL HUGO meets ISO quality standards and provides high performance.
