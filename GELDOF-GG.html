G&G INTERNATIONAL – ENGICON N.V.

Belgian sister companies Geldof (Engicon nv) and G&G International are leading suppliers of 'Integrated Steel Solutions' for the storage, handling and processing of bulk solids, liquids and gasses.

For the nuclear industry we are mainly active as supplier of pressure vessels and storage tanks (e.g. Olkiluoto).

Geldof and G&G mainly serve the global energy, oil and gas and storage and transshipment industries. Our customers include both major principals and EPC contractors.

Our scope of supply:

  • Pressure vessels
  • Storage tanks, tank farms, tank maintenance
  • Dry bulk storage and handling projects
  • Offshore foundations
  • A multitude of large, robust and/or complex engineered solutions that often require a combination of diverse technical competency

Items up 2.000 tons, 150 m length and 20 m diameter can be shipped worldwide through the seaport of Antwerp.

In the frame of the ITER project, G&G was a preselected candidate for the supply of the 7 EU vacuum vessel segments and is interested in the construction of heavy process components and solutions.
