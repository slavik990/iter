THALES ALENIA SPACE ETCA

Thales Alenia Space ETCA develops, manufactures and markets electronic and software products for space, telecommunication, security and defence and for industrial sectors.

Thales belongs to Thales, a leading industrial group serving Aerospace, Defence, and Security and Services markets worldwide, with operations in more than 50 countries and 69000 employees.

Thales Alenia Space ETCA is one of the world leaders in electrical supplying and distribution equipment for satellite platforms.
We supply various models of avionics for the Spacebus 4000 platform.
We also have at our disposal a whole range of DC/DC converters exported to the four corners of the world.

Activities and solutions include:

  • Space on board
  • Check-out systems
  • Telecommunication
  • Intelligent transport systems
  • Security and Defense
  • System Design Services
