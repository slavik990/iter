BARCO N.V.

Barco, a global technology company, designs and develops visualization solutions for a variety of selected professional markets. These include medical imaging, control rooms, defense and aerospace, media and entertainment, digital cinema, and simulation. Barco is active in over 90 countries and counts about 3.400 employees all over the world.

For its infrastructure, utilities and security markets in particular, Barco offers dedicated visualization for control rooms. This includes not only high-resolution, modular video walls, but also the necessary software to enhance collaboration and distributed data sharing over the network.

Barco can pride itself on the faith its customers have in its solutions, and can boast that our technology helps ensure energy distribution to one in two families around the world. Some of our high-profile customers include:

  • Elia, Belgium
  • UTE Norte Fluminese, Brazil
  • CEPS Prague, Czech Republic
  • PowerStream, Canada
  • CNPC, China
