PHIBO INDUSTRIES – YOUR PARTNER IN SURFACE CLEANING & CONDITIONING TECHNOLOGIES

Phibo Industries is already more than 25 years specialised in the study, development, production and sales of installations, accessories and specific consumables for the treatment, conditioning and cleaning of surfaces.

Phibo Industries stands for high performance, high quality and innovation in surface treatment machines and develops both manual cabinets in closed-loop systems, as well as completely automatic decontamination boots with remote control, in following areas:

  • Nuclear and Toxic Decontamination (NTD)
  • Surface cleaning before crack detection (Non Destructive Test)
  • Improvement of material fatigue (Shot peening)
  • Finishing and conditioning of stainless steel and other possible substrates with the SUBLIMOTIONprocess®
  • Degreasing and thorough cleaning from contaminants such as carbon deposits, rust, old paint layers, etc.
  • Selective removal of paint
  • Cleaning of contaminated moulds and extrusion dies, without erosion or damage to the substrates or surface structures
  • In-line cleaning and precision surface finishing of metal components, wires, cables, steel bands, ... (automated continuous processing)
  • ...

Numerous references in various industrial areas, such as

  • Nuclear industry,
  • Aerospace and Defence,
  • Chemical industry (e.g. rubber and plastics industry),
  • Pharmaceutical and Food industry,
  • Metal manufacturing industry (e.g. Stainless Steel industry), Aluminium industry,
  • Glass industry, Composites, Automotive,

Specific references are available on demand.
