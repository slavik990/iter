IEMANTS N.V.

Iemants N.V. is an international operating steel construction company with over 50 years of experience in the construction, fabrication, supply and assembly of steel constructions.

Iemants is specialized in heavy and technically high-grade projects.


Services:
  • design
  • calculation
  • drawing
  • fabrication
  • corrosion protection
  • erection

Products:
  • bridges
  • locks
  • buildings
  • industrial installations
  • onshore modules
  • offshore projects
  • cranes and pylons for wind turbines
  • nuclear

Together with its subsidiary Willems Staalconstructies, Iemants realizes an annual production capacity of 35.000 tonnes.

Iemants has offices in Qatar and Abu Dhabi and an engineering office in Bangalore.
