GRADEL

Gradel is specialized in engineering and manufacturing of systems and components for civil nuclear research and industry applications and has numerous references in fission and fusion projects


Gradel was founded in 1965 and has since the beginning focused on nuclear industry with a large program of equipments for remote handling, cutting, underwater cleaning and filtering installations.

Thanks to its integrated engineering office with a large experience, Gradel has the ability to assume satisfactory start-to-finish operations, thereby fulfilling the role of consultants.

Gradel is managing complete projects including engineering, manufacturing supervising, qualified welding, assembling and after sales services with aged staff in controlled zones in nuclear power plants.

The company's management concentrates on the quality of the provided customer services, by means of the support of its highly qualified staff executing continuously the improvement program conform to DIN EN ISO 9001:2000 and KTA 1401.
